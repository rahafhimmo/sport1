How to Lose Weight?

In short , it's all about the concept of calories in, calories out:
Eat less than you burn and you’ll lose weight. the most common tips are :
Eat high fibre foods , Drink plenty of water , Skip sugary beverages ,
Eat more fruits and vegetables , and do workouts.

How to Gain Weight?

Eat more than you burn ,
The most common tips for this are: Eat Plenty of Protein
(Eating sufficient protein is required to gain muscle weight instead of just fat) ,
Eat more of Carbs and Fat and , Don’t drink water before meals This can fill your stomach ,
Eat more often , and also try to workout so not all your weight be just fat but also muscles.
